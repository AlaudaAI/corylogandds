About Our Practice
Revolutionizing Smiles Since 2002

Our general and cosmetic practice in Houston, Texas has been revolutionizing smiles for Houston's Downtown, Midtown, River Oaks, Heights, West University, Bellaire and the surrounding areas since 2002. We offer a full array of dental and cosmetic dentistry services to help you maintain healthy teeth. Our office takes pride and is committed to providing the highest quality of dental care available with the personalized attention that you deserve.

We strive on excellence and provide the most comprehensive and complete care to all of our clients. Working together, we help our clients make the most informed decisions for their life long dental care.
